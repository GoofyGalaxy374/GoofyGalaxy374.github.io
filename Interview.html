An interview with the Co-Principal of PS "Ivan Vazov"

Nikolay Genchev: For how many years did you work in primary school Ivan Vazov?


Gergana Pavlova: I work here more than 20 years already.

NG: Are the children changing?

GP: Yes, they are changing with the progress of technologies.

NG: Most of our teaches have studied in our school, did you study here?

GP: Yes, I did. My class teacher was Roza Dimitrova. She was math teacher.

NG: Is it easier to be a student or a teacher?

GP: It is easier to be a student. If you are a teacher you have to be responsible with the students and the parents.
