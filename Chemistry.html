Chemistry in a nutshell

Chemistry is the scientific discipline involved with compounds composed of atoms ,elements, and molecules, combinations of atoms. The atom is considered to be the main building block of the substance and consists of a dense central portion called an atomic nucleus which has a positive electric charge surrounded by a cloud of electrons with a negative electrical charge. The molecule is a more or less stable combination of a number of atoms linked by chemical bonds. Molecules are considered neutral and all valences are saturated. The molecule, which consists of a huge number of atoms, is called the macromolecule. In Bulgaria Chemistry started learning in 7 grade.
